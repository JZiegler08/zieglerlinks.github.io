The final Project we created this year was a video project. For the video, our goal was to create a promotional video that promotes a building on the Millersville campus. For our video we decided to focus on the student memorial center. This is the hub for all student activity on campus.The student memorial center is where the Millersville rec center, galley dining hall, University bookstore, and more are kept. For this project we started off by creating a storyboard. After that was approved we had to start getting film for the video. We recorded our audio and video separate so the audio from the camera was not too quiet. We then used CapCut to combine the video and audio clips and create our final product.
