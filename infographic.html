Jesse Ziegler Portfolio for AENG 110
                   
















This Project was an infographic we created based on past technologies. This infographic was created in Indesign and the images were pulled from the internet. For my infographic I chose the VHS. For this project I talked about the creation of the vhs, How it works, and the impact it has had on the world. The goal of this project was to show how past technologies show the communication technologies model. To begin this project each student had to pick a communication technology and post our decision on D2L. We then had to research the technology we decided on to get a better understanding of the history and how it works. After that we had to create our visual display and then print the final product out.
